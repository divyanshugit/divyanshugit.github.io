The goal of this challenge was to develop ML models that could rate the complexity of reading passages for Grade 3-12 classroom use. The evaluation metrics RMSE(In this we calculate the mean square root of the difference between original and predicted values).

I used an ensemble of three transformer models was constructed, which included two state-of-the-art pre-trained models for natural language processing: BERT and Roberta.
