### How to Write New Blog Posts:

Create a new markdown file in `src/blog/` with this frontmatter:

```yaml
---
layout: blog-post.njk
title: "Your Blog Title"
description: "Your blog description"
date: 2025-MM-DD
tags:
  - Tag1
  - Tag2
emailSubject: Your Email Subject
featured: true/false
permalink: /blog/your-url-slug.html
references:
  1: https://reference1.com
  2: https://reference2.com
---

Your content here with [1,2] inline references...
```
